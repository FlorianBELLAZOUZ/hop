ECMAScript 6 support
====================

 * arrow functions
 * `let` and `const` bindings.
 * symbols.
 * template strings (without `raw` object on first argument).
   [ECMAScript 6][es6-template-tagged], [ECMAScript 6][es6-template-literals],
   [MDN][mdn-template]
